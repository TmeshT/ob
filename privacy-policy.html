Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top priority.

1. Introduction

At MyBank, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data when you visit our online banking system or use our services. By using our platform, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our system:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, email, phone number, national ID, date of birth.
  • Banking Details: Account numbers, transaction history, payment information.
  • Device and Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you use our website or mobile app, including clickstream data and page visits.

3. How We Use Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our banking services.
  • Process transactions securely.
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud.
  • Improve system functionality, security, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data:

  • With regulatory bodies when legally required.
  • With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption and stored on secure servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.
